What is Our Purpose?

Many people across the world need help managing their money and their finances.
However, the very people that need help are the ones who need help are the ones that cannot afford to hire a financial planner.
Our goal at Investacopia is to provide free financial planning and guidance in order to help those that are economically disadvantaged.
We believe that basic education regarding how to protect ones wealth and money should be accessable to everyone for free and without a price to pay.
