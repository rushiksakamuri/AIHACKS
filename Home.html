What is Our Purpose?

Many people across the world need help managing their money
and their finances. However, the very people that need help
are the ones who need help are the ones that cannot afford
to hire a financial planner. Our goal at Investacopia is to
provide free financial planning and guidance in order to help
those that are economically disadvantaged. We believe that basic
education regarding how to protect ones wealth and money should be
accessable to everyone for free and without a price to pay.

What can Investacopia do for you?

Investacopia acts as a financial planner whose responses are generated
through artifical intelligence. Input your income and costs and we can
create a financial plan best suited for you. We can help evaluate how much
money you should be saving, investing, and spending based on the lifestyle
you live and the one you want to live. Let us help you live a more comfortable
life without constantly stressing over your finances!

Skip the tedious work and stress of financial planning and let us take care
of it for you! Save money so that you can spend it on the things and people
you love. We aim to make everyone's life easier and bring joy to the faces
of many people! Maybe now you'll be able to go on that vacation you've always
wanted to go to.
